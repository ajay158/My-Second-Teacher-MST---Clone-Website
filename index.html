Interactive Videos

Transform your teaching and learning experience with our fun interactive video lessons for Grade 11 and 12, designed for an engaging, enjoyable, and easy-to-understand educational experience.

Classroom

Conduct hassle-free virtual classes on mySecondTeacher’s Classroom feature. Create, schedule, and conduct classes with ease.

Testpaper

Teachers can effortlessly create custom test papers on a topic, subject or the entire curriculum using our Testpapers feature. Teachers can also access a secret test bank to evaluate their students’ progress. Students can create Testpapers and evaluate their own understandings and shortcomings.

Group Chat

Connect and exchange ideas easily using our group chat feature for students and teachers. Organize projects, receive updates, and collaborate effortlessly by effectively communicating within these groups.

Reposts and Statistics

Maximise student success with our Mastery Reports. Analyse assessment reports, Testpaper scores, and attendance records to identify learning gaps and take timely corrective actions.

Quizzes & Simulations

Our highly interactive in-lesson and end-of-lesson quizzes help students get critical insights like never before! These simulations provide specific and overall insights into the learning behaviour of each student and entire classes via Diagnostic Reports.

Online Platform Support

Experience seamless support with our online technical assistance available from 6 AM to 10 PM. We ensure prompt and effective solutions for all your technical queries and issues.
